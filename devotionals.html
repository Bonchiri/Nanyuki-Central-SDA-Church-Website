The Quiet Assurance of Trust

                   

"Trust in the Lord forever, for the Lord God is an everlasting rock." — Isaiah 26:4 (NIV)

                   
                       

In a world of shifting sands and unpredictable storms, finding a steady anchor is not just comforting—it's essential for survival. Isaiah reminds us where that anchor lies: in the **unchanging nature of our everlasting God**. Today, take a moment to pause your anxieties and consciously place your trust in Him. This isn't a passive trust; it's an active decision to rely on the Rock.

                       

The prophet's call to "trust in the Lord forever" is a deep invitation to rely on God's character, not just His capacity. He is the ultimate **Rock, immutable and dependable**. When we choose to trust Him, the burdens of uncertainty lift, replaced by a quiet, unshakable assurance. May your faith be settled on the solid Rock today.

                   
                   
                       

Contributor: Elder David Njoroge
