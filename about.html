                                Our Story and Beliefs            

           

Nanyuki Central SDA is a community rooted in faith, committed to serving God and our neighbors.

       
       
           
               

Our History

               

Nanyuki Central SDA Church traces its roots back to the early days of Adventist outreach in Kenya. Established by dedicated pioneers who carried the message of hope and health, our congregation has grown into a vibrant center of worship and community service. We stand today on the foundations of the Bible and the prophetic message, continuing the mission to share the eternal gospel in our generation.

           
           
               

Our Guiding Principles

               
                   
                       

Mission

                       

To call people to become disciples of Jesus Christ, to proclaim the everlasting gospel, and to prepare the world for His soon return.

                   
                   
                       

Vision

                       

To be a Christ-centered community, reflecting His love and character through holistic ministry, touching lives in Nanyuki and beyond.
